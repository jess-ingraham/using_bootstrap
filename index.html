Who We Are

Emily's Diner was first founded by Grandma Emily, as she was known to the town. She started the diner when people began asking her for recipes and regularly coming to her house for food. She started the diner out of her own kitchen as a delivery service until she saved up enough money to buy the building Emily's Diner is in today. Much of the building has been perserved, as well as the recipes, as well as it could. Some renovation has been done over time but we have stayed true to Grandma Emily. We are family owned and understand the importance of building relationships...especially through food.

Our Philosophy

We believe comfort food should always remind you of home. And we believe that comfort food can be healthy. All of our produce, meat, dairy, vegetables are sourced locally, and bought fresh from family farms. We believe in the relationship between farmer and chef, as well as between you and your family and friends. So whether it's a birthday party, family get-together, or just lunch, we gaurentee the best comfort food from the best people.
